# Public Release Sequence and Audit Queue

This file separates three different orders that should not be confused:

1. **Intellectual/dependency order** — how the programme is built.
2. **Public reader order** — how an outside reader should enter the corpus.
3. **Suitability cleanup order** — which files should be audited first for public release.

BVD and GBS are core methodology papers, but they are late-stage methodology papers. They should not be presented as early foundations.

## 2. Paper A and Paper B classification

They are better classified as:

```text

```

## 5. Why BVD was audited early

Bridge-Valence Diagnostics was audited early as a **hard-case test** for the suitability protocol. That does not mean it comes first in the programme sequence.

BVD and GBS should be handled carefully because they are dense and methodology-critical. But public release should still explain that they arrive after the foundation and bridge-calibration stack.

## 6. Rule

Do not confuse:

```text
publishable / externally interesting
```

with:

```text
foundation-driving / dependency-prior
```

Paper A and Paper B may be among the most publishable papers, but they are not foundation drivers. BVD and GBS are core methodology, but they are not early foundations.
