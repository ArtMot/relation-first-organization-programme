# Public Release Sequence and Audit Queue

This file separates four different orders that should not be confused:

1. **Intellectual/dependency order** — how the programme is built.
2. **Public reader order** — how an outside reader should enter the corpus.
3. **Suitability cleanup order** — which files should be audited first for public release.
4. **Publishability order** — which externally legible papers may be easiest to submit to journals or arXiv.

BVD and GBS are core methodology papers, but they are late-stage methodology papers. They should not be presented as early foundations.

Paper A and Paper B may be externally publishable, but they are not foundation drivers. Charted Objecthood is not merely an application paper; it is a formal objecthood/gluing foundation paper with contextuality as a specialization. Persistence Margins and Moving Supports are external-facing theorem/application papers. No Undeclared Photon is a NUR/NUB/NUO case study. No-Free-Transfer is a foundational bridge/no-go predecessor that must be checked for carry-forward into No Undeclared Relation.

## 3. Additional classification corrections

### 3.1 Charted Objecthood

Charted Objecthood and Contextual Gluing should be treated as a **formal foundation/objecthood paper**, not merely an external application.

It asks when chart-relative proper organizations count as one object. Its contextuality material is a specialization / bridge-realization, not the whole role of the paper.

Recommended role:

```text
Foundation / formal objecthood and gluing layer; contextuality specialization.
```

### 3.2 No-Free-Transfer

No-Free-Transfer and Bridge Completion should be treated as a **foundational bridge/no-go predecessor** to No Undeclared Relation.

It may be superseded by No Undeclared Relation, but it should not be dismissed until a carry-forward audit confirms that NUR preserves all relevant NFT content.

Recommended role:

```text
Bridge/no-go foundation predecessor; include in version lineage or public corpus depending on carry-forward audit.
```

### 3.3 No Undeclared Photon

No Undeclared Photon is not a foundation paper and not a photon derivation.

Recommended role:

```text
NUR/NUB/NUO case study using the photon as subject; physics-facing relation-packet exemplar.
```

### 3.4 Persistence Margins / Moving Supports

Persistence Margins and Moving Supports are external-facing theorem/application papers.

Recommended role:

```text
TDA-adjacent theorem/application notes showing RFOP can produce bounded quantitative results.
```

## 6. Why BVD was audited early

Bridge-Valence Diagnostics was audited early as a **hard-case test** for the suitability protocol. That does not mean it comes first in the programme sequence.

BVD and GBS should be handled carefully because they are dense and methodology-critical. But public release should still explain that they arrive after the foundation and bridge-calibration stack.

## 7. Rule

Do not confuse:

```text
publishable / externally interesting
```

with:

```text
foundation-driving / dependency-prior
```

Paper A and Paper B may be among the most publishable papers, but they are not foundation drivers. BVD and GBS are core methodology, but they are not early foundations. Charted Objecthood is a foundation/objecthood paper with an external contextuality specialization. No Undeclared Photon is a case study. Persistence Margins and Moving Supports are external theorem/application notes.
