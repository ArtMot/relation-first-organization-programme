## Summary judgment

FRK is a late foundation paper. It sharpens the relation-first triadic requirement into a kernel calculus: boundary, mediation, visibility, coherence, and residual. It should be part of the foundation-completion stack after BVD/GBS or beside them in the late-methodology foundation layer.

## Strengths

- Clear central kernel object.
- Strong staged theorem hierarchy.
- Good distinction between programme-native construction and external mathematics.
- Good non-claims: it does not claim to derive all mathematics, thermodynamics, or physics.
- Useful discriminating negatives that prevent overclaiming from valid partial constructions.
- Strong reader orientation and dependency summary.

## Main suitability issues

1. Draft subtitle is too internal and should move to metadata/provenance.
2. Title should expose the kernel content, not only the internal term.
3. Reference checks are needed for the external traditions named in the orientation section.
4. The synchronization and thermodynamics examples need scope guarding so they remain conditional external realizations.
5. Because FRK depends on BVD/GBS, the public reader map should make its late-foundation position explicit.

## Release decision

Likely public-suitable after title/front-matter cleanup, references, and example-scope check.

**Decision:** release-ready with minor edits if references and scope guardrails are stable; otherwise content-preserving public revision needed.
